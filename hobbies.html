My Hobbies

I have a variety of hobbies and ways that I like to spend my time. I very often say yes to things, and like trying new activities.

My hobbies include:

  • Reading - I like reading all sorts of books, but my main genres are science fiction, philosophy, auto-biographies and fantasy
  • Sports - I like to play badminton and tennis, and I am soon to start training for boxing for charity. I have also played on football and basketball teams in the past.
  • Climbing - I enjoy climbing and have done it both indoor and outdoor. I tend to do bouldering more than climbing though.
  • Travel - I love traveling. I have visited quite a few countries in Europe and South East Asia. I spent a year traveling SE Asia from 2019 - 2020
  • Gaming - I enjoy gaming on occasion when I have spare time. My favourite genre of games at RPGs.
